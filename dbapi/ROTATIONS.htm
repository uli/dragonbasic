ROTATIONS Subroutines & Functions

COS# - Look up the cosine of an angle.
MAKEROTATION - Setup one of the 4 available rotation matrices.
ROTATESPRITE - Enables the rotation of a sprite.
SIN# - Look up the sine of an angle.
TAN# - Computes the tangent of an angle.
WRAPANGLE - Clamps an angle to [0,359].
