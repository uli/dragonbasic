SOUND :: TURNSOUNDOFF

Disables all sounds. 
SUB TURNSOUNDOFF
Parameters
None.
Return
None.
Remarks
Call this to turn off the sound chip on the GBA. This will instantly halt any currently playing music or sounds (although they will still be processed).
