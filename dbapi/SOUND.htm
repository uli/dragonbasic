SOUND Subroutines & Functions

PLAYDRUM - Emits white noise from sound channel 4.
PLAYMUSIC - Play a looping, 8-bit, WAV sample.
PLAYNOTE - Plays a single note.
PLAYSOUND - Play an 8-bit, WAV sample one time.
STOPMUSIC - Stops the currently running music sample.
STOPSOUND - Stops the currently running sound sample.
TURNSOUNDOFF - Disables all sounds.
TURNSOUNDON - Enables all sounds.
